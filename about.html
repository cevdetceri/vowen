Our Story

Where it began

VOWEN was born from a love of craft. Founded in London by a team of designers and artisans who believed that wedding accessories deserved the same care as the dress itself — we began making pieces for friends, then for brides across the country.

Each piece passes through many pairs of hands before it reaches yours. We source freshwater pearls from sustainable farms, work with recycled gold and silver, and use silk from certified mills. The process is slow. It's meant to be.

Today, VOWEN pieces have been worn at hundreds of weddings — on cathedrals, clifftops, and kitchen gardens. We remain a small studio by choice. Every order is handled with the same attention as the first.

Guiding Principles

What We Believe In

Slow making

We never rush production. Each piece takes the time it needs, however long that is.

Honest materials

We use only what we can trace. Recycled metals, sustainable pearls, certified silk.

Lasting design

We design for the wedding day and the decades that follow. No trends. Only pieces you'll treasure.
