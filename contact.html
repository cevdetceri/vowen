Appointment FAQs

Can I bring someone with me?

Yes, you're welcome to bring one guest. Our studio is designed as an intimate, private space — an extra pair of eyes (and honest opinions) is always welcome.

What should I wear?

Comfortable clothing you can move in — we'll do the rest. If you have a particular silhouette in mind, nude or skin-toned underlayers can be helpful.

Can I cancel or reschedule?

Yes, up to 48 hours before your appointment. Simply email us at hello@vowen.com and we'll be happy to find another time that suits you.
