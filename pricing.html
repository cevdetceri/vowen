Common Questions

Most bespoke commissions take 6–8 weeks from your initial consultation to delivery. More complex pieces — such as our Archive tier sets — may take up to 10 weeks. We'll always give you a clear timeline before we begin, and our dedicated studio liaison keeps you updated at every stage.
Yes — we ship worldwide. Every order is sent via a tracked, insured courier and packed with our signature tissue, ribbon, and care card. International delivery times vary by destination, but we'll send you full tracking details as soon as your piece leaves our studio.
Because every bespoke piece is made entirely for you, we're unable to accept returns. That said, your happiness is our absolute priority. We work closely with you through every revision round, and we won't close the commission until you're completely satisfied with the result.
We source only the finest materials: freshwater and baroque pearls, Swarovski and hand-cut crystals, 18k gold-filled and sterling silver wire, pure silk tulle and chiffon, and hand-pressed botanicals. All materials are ethically sourced. For bespoke commissions, a sample pack is available with our Collection and Archive tiers so you can feel each option before we begin.
Yes. For orders over £300 we offer a 3-instalment payment plan at no extra cost: one third at booking, one third at the midpoint approval stage, and the final third before dispatch. Simply mention this when you get in touch and we'll set it up for you.
Each piece comes with a printed care card tailored to its materials. In general: store in the dust bag provided, away from direct sunlight and humidity; avoid contact with perfume, hairspray, and oils; clean pearls gently with a dry soft cloth. Silk veils should be steamed on a low setting, never ironed. For metal components, a silver polishing cloth will keep them bright.
Absolutely. Our In-Studio Visit and Full Bridal Styling appointments both give you the opportunity to handle and try our full range in our London studio. If you're not local, our Virtual Consultation includes a curated look-book and we can arrange short-term loan samples for a small refundable deposit.
Yes — every VOWEN order arrives beautifully presented as standard. Each piece is wrapped in soft tissue, nestled in our signature ivory gift box, and tied with a satin ribbon. A handwritten note card is included on request. No additional charge for gift presentation.
